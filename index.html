Bio

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Texas A&M University, where I conduct research in the Data Integration, Visualization, and Exploration (DIVE) Laboratory under the supervision of Shuiwang Ji. My research lies at the intersection of mechanical engineering, computational physics, and machine learning, with a focus on high-order numerical schemes for fluid dynamics and the development of physics-aware, data-driven models for complex flows and mechanical systems. I received my Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology.
